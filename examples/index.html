Flocking

Have you ever wondered why birds flocks together? Is it that there's a special leader bird, or is it possible that beautiful patterns emerge from simple interactions. Use this model to experiment with flocking. The avoid block tells a bird to move away from its closest neighbor, towards tells a bird to turn in the direction of its closest neighbors, and align tells a bird to try to match the flying direction of other birds around it.
